Dr. Mohammad Rahjoo is a researcher and founder specializing in solar energy, thermal energy storage (TES), heat transfer, computational fluid dynamics (CFD), machine learning, and artificial intelligence. His research focuses on developing innovative solutions for energy efficiency and renewable energy integration.

With a PhD in Nanostructured Physics and Advanced Materials and an MBA in Entrepreneurship, he brings a multidisciplinary approach spanning academic research and product development. His academic background also includes an MSc in Energy Conversion and a BSc in Heat & Fluid (Mechanical Engineering).

Currently, he leads ProExergy, building AI-powered HVAC and building energy APIs for equipment sizing, exergy analysis, and energy calculations.
